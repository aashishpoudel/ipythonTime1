My Story

Hi! I am Aashish.
I started iPythonTime to make coding welcoming for kids everywhere.

I have created a customized course on Python from years of my teaching experience.
My approach blends creativity, structure, and encouragement.

Mission

My mission is to pass on the skills and knowledge I've honed in industry to help kids think clearly and build confidently. I work towards translating real-world Python into real student projects.

About Myself

I am a Software Engineer. I have spent last decade-plus building real-world systems with Python — everything from test-automation tools/frameworks and firmware validation to data analytics and dashboards in the cloud. These experiences come from my academic and industry career experience at world-leading university/technology companies.

I have had the opportunity to work across all levels of programming — from low-level languages like C/C++, to web development with JavaScript/HTML and SQL, to scripting with Perl and Python, to system design in MATLAB, to design GUI based MIS system in C#.NET

These collective experiences and illustrious career have shaped how I explain complex ideas simply.
